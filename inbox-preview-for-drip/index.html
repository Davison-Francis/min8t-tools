What image size limit does Drip enforce?

Drip accepts uploads up to 1000 KB per image and recommends a max width of 600px for email body width. Supported formats: JPG, PNG, GIF.

Does Drip verify SPF, DKIM, and DMARC automatically?

Drip requires SPF and DKIM via DNS. DMARC policy is your DNS configuration.
